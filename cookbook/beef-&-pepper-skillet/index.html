beef & pepper skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef (90% lean)
  • 1 can (14-1/2 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 2 serrano peppers
  • 25oz beef broth
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1.5 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 cups instant brown rice
  • 1 medium sweet red pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium green pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese

Directions

  1. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink, 6-8 minutes, breaking into crumbles; drain.
  2. Add tomatoes, broth, chili powder, salt, and garlic powder; bring to a boil.
  3. Stir in rice and peppers.
  4. Reduce heat; simmer, covered, until liquid is absorbed, 8-10 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat; salt to taste
