crockpot honey garlic chicken noodles

ingredients

  • 400g chicken breast
  • 20g dark soy sauce
  • 15g sriracha sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 25g garlic paste
  • 8g ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp corn flour
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 100ml chicken stock
  • 200g protein noodles

instructions

  1. add all the ingredients to the crockpot
  2. cook on high for 4 hours, then on low for 8 hours
