hamburger cauliflower casserole

ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 large sweet onion, diced
  • 6 medium carrots, cut into bite-size pieces
  • ¾ head cauliflower, chopped into bite-size pieces
  • 30 fresh green beans, ends clipped, and beans cut into 1” pieces
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • ⅓ cup dry bread crumbs

instructions

  1. preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. heat a large pot over medium-high heat. cook and stir the ground beef until browned, adding the onions the last few minutes of cooking. drain the fat into a container to cool and discard.
  3. into the ground beef, stir in the carrots, cauliflower, and green beans.
  4. mix the milk, egg, and seasoning blend in a small bowl. pour over the meat and vegetable mixture in the pot and mix.
  5. carefully pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish. sprinkle the bread crumbs on top and mix slightly to distribute the crumbs.
  6. cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  7. bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. uncover and return to the oven.
  8. continue baking for 15 minutes.
  9. serves 6.
