bachelor's fried broccoli

  • olive oil (or vegetable oil)

  • soy sauce

  • seasoned soy sauce

  • sweetened soy sauce

  • fry

  • fry the broccoli in oil until it is golden brown and almost soft

    • only add enough olive oil to coat the broccoli, but not add a puddle

  • add soy sauces 7 regular: 2 sweet: 1 seasoning to the pan, and continue adding in that ratio so that the bottom of the pan never completely dried up until the broccoli is fully cooked
